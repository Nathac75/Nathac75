The Game

This is a page about a tactical first person shooting game made by Riot Games. Currently it is free to play with the option to purchase weapon skins, sprays, and namecards. Valorant has several gamemodes currently the only permanent ones are unrated, competitive, and spike rush. They do have some games in rotation such as escalation, replication, and snowball fight.

The Competition

As Valorant grows as a game, comeptitive scene is also rapidly growing often being compared to the massively popular game Counter Strike because of it's genre in Tactical FPS. Riot Productions developed the Valorant Challengers tour which is hosted in the regions known as NA, SA, EMEA, APAC, KR, BR, JPN, and LATAM. Each of these regions being decided based seemingly the size of the Valorant scene in it's respective location.

The format of the VCT has been alive for 2 years.
